## Book 2 - Building Local-First Systems - Chap 2 - The Illusion of 'Offline Support'

Many applications claim to support offline usage, but what they actually provide is temporary caching layered on top of a fundamentally network-dependent system. This chapter examines why most “offline modes” fail under real-world conditions and clarifies the difference between cache-based resilience and true local-first architecture.
