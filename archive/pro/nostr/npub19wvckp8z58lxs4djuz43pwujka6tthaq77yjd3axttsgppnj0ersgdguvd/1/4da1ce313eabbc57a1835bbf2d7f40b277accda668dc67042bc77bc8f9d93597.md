6/25/2026 — Identity Before Applications

The more I work on Continuum, the less I think about applications.

Applications come and go.

Identity persists.

If I change browsers, operating systems, Nostr clients, or even protocols, I don't want to rebuild who I am each time.

Software should adapt to my identity.

Not the other way around.
