# StartNation Micro Startup Operations Setup
($399)

This package provides a ready-to-run operational and financial infrastructure for early-stage micro startups (especially AI-native startups with 1–3 operators). The goal is to help founders move quickly from idea to revenue while maintaining clean financial records and operational discipline from the start.


---

# 1. Payment System Setup (Stripe)

Stripe provides the core payment infrastructure for most micro startups.

Deliverables include:

- Stripe account configuration  
- Product or service pricing setup  
- Payment links or checkout pages  
- Basic billing configuration  
- Integration with accounting system  

This enables founders to accept payments immediately without needing custom development.

---

# 2. Accounting System Setup (QuickBooks)

QuickBooks serves as the financial record system for the startup.

Deliverables include:

- Account creation and configuration  
- Chart of accounts setup  
- Stripe integration  
- Invoice template configuration  
- Financial reporting setup  

QuickBooks becomes the system of record for revenue, expenses, and financial reporting.

---

# 3. Expense Tracking System (Expensify)

Expensify allows founders to easily track and categorize expenses without manual bookkeeping.

Deliverables include:

- Expensify account setup  
- Receipt capture workflow  
- Expense categorization structure  
- Integration with QuickBooks  

This ensures that expenses are captured and categorized automatically as the business operates.

---

# 4. Invoicing System (QuickBooks)

Invoices can be generated and tracked directly through QuickBooks.

Deliverables include:

- Invoice template setup  
- Client invoicing workflow  
- Payment tracking  
- Integration with Stripe payments  

This ensures that founders can easily invoice clients and monitor outstanding payments.

---

# 5. Revenue Dashboard (QuickBooks)

QuickBooks can provide a simple but effective revenue dashboard that allows founders to monitor financial performance.

Example dashboard metrics:

Monthly revenue  
Monthly expenses  
Net profit  
Outstanding invoices  
Top customers  

This gives founders immediate visibility into the financial health of the startup.

---

# 6. Tax-Ready Bookkeeping Structure

This deliverable sets up the accounting structure so that taxes can be filed without needing to rebuild the company’s books later.

Most early startups create accounting chaos by categorizing expenses inconsistently or failing to structure their revenue and expense categories properly. This setup provides a clean chart of accounts and standardized financial categories.

Example chart of accounts structure:

Revenue

- Product revenue  
- Consulting revenue  
- Subscription revenue  

Expenses

- Software subscriptions  
- AI tools (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)  
- Marketing and advertising  
- Contractors and freelancers  
- Travel  
- Equipment and office expenses  

Additional setup may include:

- QuickBooks chart of accounts configuration  
- Expense categorization rules  
- Stripe to QuickBooks synchronization settings  
- Sales tax settings where applicable  
- Instructions for generating accountant-ready financial reports  

The objective is simple: when tax season arrives, the company’s financial records are already structured correctly.

# 7. Operational Checklist & Workflow

This deliverable provides a documented playbook describing how the operational tools work together and how the founder should run the business day-to-day.

Most founders install tools like Stripe or QuickBooks but never integrate them into a coherent workflow. This component provides the step-by-step processes that connect payment processing, accounting, expense tracking, and reporting.

Example payment workflow:

1. Client signs contract or agrees to services  
2. Invoice generated in QuickBooks  
3. Payment processed via Stripe  
4. Stripe transaction syncs to QuickBooks  
5. Revenue appears in dashboard  
6. Payment reconciled during monthly review  

Example expense workflow:

1. Expense submitted in Expensify  
2. Receipt attached  
3. Expense categorized  
4. Synced to QuickBooks  
5. Included in profit/loss reporting  

Example monthly founder checklist:

Monthly Operations Checklist

1. Reconcile Stripe payments  
2. Review expense categories  
3. Generate profit and loss statement  
4. Verify outstanding invoices  
5. Export tax-ready reports  
6. Review revenue dashboard  

This operational playbook ensures that founders understand exactly how the tools interact and how to maintain a clean financial record as the company grows.
