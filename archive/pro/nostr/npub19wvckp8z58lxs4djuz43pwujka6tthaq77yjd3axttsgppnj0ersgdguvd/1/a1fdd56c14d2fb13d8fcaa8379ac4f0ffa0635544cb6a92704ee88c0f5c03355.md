Sabbath Reflection: Writing Without Permission - 3/7/2026

Summary: Local-first tools allow creators to write without waiting for platforms or networks.

One of the things I appreciate about Continuum is that writing does not require permission.

There is no server that must be reachable.

No platform that must approve the post.

No account system that must authenticate you.

You can simply write.

If the network is available, the event can be published. If it is not, the event can wait.

But the act of writing itself is never blocked.

That small design choice restores something that the modern internet slowly took away: independence.

And that independence feels particularly appropriate to reflect on today.
