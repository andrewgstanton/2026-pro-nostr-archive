Decentralizing the protocol doesn’t automatically change the culture.

If the interfaces, incentives, and behaviors stay the same,
you get the same patterns — just on a different rail.

More freedom at the base layer doesn’t guarantee more depth at the social layer.

That’s not a criticism of the protocol.

It’s a reminder that culture doesn’t upgrade automatically.

Changing the foundation matters.

But it doesn’t change people overnight.
