Architecture Is Theology - 3/2/2026





Every system embeds a view of man.

If your architecture assumes trustlessness, it reflects realism about human nature.

If your architecture assumes centralized authority, it reflects dependency.

Bitcoin assumes we cannot blindly trust.  
Nostr assumes identity should not require permission.

These are not technical decisions alone. They are philosophical commitments embedded in code.

Traditional web architecture assumes:
- Centralized identity authority
- Mutable data controlled by platform owners
- Credential storage as a normal liability

Self-sovereign architecture assumes:
- Identity should be portable
- Keys should be locally controlled
- Data should be verifiable independently of platform approval

What does your software assume about humanity?

Engineering that ignores human nature collapses under it.

Architecture is theology expressed in infrastructure.
