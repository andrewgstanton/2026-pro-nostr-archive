## 🔒 Book 2 - Building Local-First Systems - Chap 6 - Local Authority First

A local-first system must treat the local environment as the primary source of truth. This is not a conceptual preference but a design constraint that shapes storage, identity, synchronization, and failure handling. This chapter defines what “local authority” means in concrete terms and how to implement it in practice.
