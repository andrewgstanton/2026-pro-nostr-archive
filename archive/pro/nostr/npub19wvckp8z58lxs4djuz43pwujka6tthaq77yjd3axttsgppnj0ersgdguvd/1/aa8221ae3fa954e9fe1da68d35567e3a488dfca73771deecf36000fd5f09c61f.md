I published a new article today that explores the question that eventually led me to build Continuum.

The article is a short companion to Chapter 1 of Building Continuum. It tells the same story from a different angle and asks a simple question:

What if your digital home wasn't an application?

Sometimes a five-minute article is enough to introduce an idea. Sometimes it takes a book to explore where that idea leads.
