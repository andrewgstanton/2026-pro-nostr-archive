## A Focused Way to See What Can Break Before It Actually Does

Most teams don’t fail because they lack talent or effort.  
They fail because hidden constraints surface too late.

I offer **short, fixed-scope architecture and risk reviews** for teams building complex systems — especially where identity, data ownership, AI, or long-term durability matter.

This is not a long-term contract and not a hiring funnel.  
It’s a time-boxed intervention designed to surface risks early and clarify tradeoffs.

### What This Is
A **2–3 week engagement** focused on understanding how your system actually behaves under pressure.

Typical areas of review include:
- system architecture and boundaries
- identity and key management
- data ownership and exit paths
- AI integration points and leakage risks
- long-term failure modes (technical and incentive-driven)

### What You Get
- a written assessment
- clearly identified risk hotspots
- tradeoff analysis (what you gain vs what you give up)
- concrete recommendations you can act on — or ignore

No implementation obligation.  
No dependency created.

### What This Is Not
- not a rewrite
- not staff augmentation
- not a resume-driven role
- not an open-ended advisory relationship

The goal is clarity, not capture.

### Who This Is For
- early-stage teams shipping real systems
- founders making irreversible architectural decisions
- organizations worried about long-term risk, not just velocity
- teams navigating AI, identity, or local-first constraints

### Engagement Details
- Fixed scope
- Fixed timeline
- Clear start and end
- Typical range: **$3k–$8k**, depending on complexity
