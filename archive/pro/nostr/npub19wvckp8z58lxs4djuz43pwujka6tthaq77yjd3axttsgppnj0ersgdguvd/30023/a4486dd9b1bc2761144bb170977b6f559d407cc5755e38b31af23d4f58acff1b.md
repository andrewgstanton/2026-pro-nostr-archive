## 🔒 Book 2 - Building Local-First Systems - Chap. 9 - Separation of Authoring and Distribution

Most systems tightly couple the act of creating content with the act of publishing it. This creates dependency on networks, platforms, and infrastructure at the moment of creation. Local-first systems must separate these concerns. This chapter defines the distinction between authoring and distribution and explains how decoupling them enables durability, flexibility, and true sovereignty.
