## 🔒 Book 2 - Building Local-First Systems - Chap 7 - Determinism Over Dynamism

Local-first systems prioritize determinism — predictable, reproducible behavior — over dynamic, server-driven state. This chapter explains why determinism is essential for trust, recoverability, and durability, and how it shapes system design choices around state, execution, and synchronization.
