## What I Am Actually Offering

Not a tool.

Not “Nostr onboarding.”

Not a tech experiment.

I am offering structured Sovereign Digital Continuity for ministries.

---

## The Core Problem Ministries Face

- Dependence on centralized platforms
- Risk of deplatforming or shadow suppression
- Fragmented archives
- No custody of communication identity
- Leadership transitions with no key continuity
- Donor communication tied to third-party systems

Most ministries do not have a digital sovereignty plan.

They have accounts.

Accounts are not custody.

---

## What I Offer

### 1. Identity Custody Setup

- Establish a dedicated ministry identity (`npub`)
- Key custody plan (who holds what, how backed up)
- Separation of roles (leadership, communications, archive)

---

### 2. Durable Publishing Workflow

- Profile setup (kind:0)
- Signed notes (kind:1)
- Long-form statements (kind:30023)
- Save + sign before publishing
- Optional relay distribution

Ministries gain the ability to create signed communications independent of platforms.

---

### 3. Archive & Continuity Strategy

- Full local mirror of ministry content
- Version retention
- Export flexibility
- Off-site backup guidance
- Leadership transition planning (key continuity)

This ensures communications survive beyond platforms or individuals.

---

### 4. Platform Independence Guidance

- Relay strategy
- Redundancy planning
- Optional cross-posting workflows
- Risk assessment discussion

The goal is not to abandon platforms.
The goal is to remove dependency.

---

### 5. Training & Support

- 2–3 live onboarding sessions
- Q&A window (30 days)
- Strategic advisory discussion on digital continuity

---

Ministries invest heavily in:
