6/25/2026 — Continuum Was Never Meant to Be Another Nostr Client

There are already excellent Nostr clients.

That was never really the problem I wanted to solve.

Continuum is using Nostr as infrastructure, not as a destination.

Publishing notes, articles, profiles, and interacting socially are important, but they're only part of a much larger picture.

I'm interested in giving people ownership over their identities and data, regardless of which application they happen to be using.
