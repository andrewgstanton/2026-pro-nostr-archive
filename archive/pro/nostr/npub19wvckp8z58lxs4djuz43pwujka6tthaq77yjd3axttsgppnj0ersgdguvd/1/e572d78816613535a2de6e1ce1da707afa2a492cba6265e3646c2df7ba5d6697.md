I keep thinking about how much of life is lived before anything is visible. 4/4/2026

We want the shoot breaking through the frost.

We want the stone rolled away.

We want visible growth.

But so much of what is real happens first beneath the surface.

Roots deepen in silence.

Life forms where no one can yet see it.

Sometimes the waiting itself is part of the work.

I have waited a long time.
