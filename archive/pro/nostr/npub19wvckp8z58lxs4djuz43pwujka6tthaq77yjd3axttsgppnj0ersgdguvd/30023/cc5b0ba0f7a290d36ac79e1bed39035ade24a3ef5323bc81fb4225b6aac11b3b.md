Future of Work Study
March 9 – 10:00 AM

---

## Preparation Time

You do **not** need to spend hours preparing.

This is a qualitative research interview. The goal is to capture real perspectives from people working with AI.

15–20 minutes of preparation is sufficient.

Focus on a few clear themes rather than detailed technical explanations.

---

# Core Perspective (30-second framing)

AI dramatically lowers the cost of producing knowledge work.

The real bottlenecks become:

- judgment
- trust
- coordination
- identity

The future of work will likely revolve around individuals or small teams orchestrating networks of AI tools and agents.

---

# Four Major Shifts

## 1. Solo Builders Become Viable Companies

AI tools reduce the operational overhead of starting and running a business.

Individuals can now perform work that previously required entire teams.

Examples:

- AI-assisted coding
- AI marketing tools
- AI research assistants
- AI customer support

Result:

Small "one-person companies" become viable.

---

## 2. Knowledge Work Becomes AI-Augmented

AI will change how work is done before it fully replaces roles.

Many professionals will become:

- reviewers
- editors
- curators
- decision makers

Instead of producing raw output.

Examples:

- engineers reviewing AI-generated code
- lawyers reviewing AI-generated briefs
- analysts verifying AI-generated research

---

## 3. Trust and Identity Become Critical

If AI can generate:

- articles
- research
- software
- media

Then **trust becomes scarce**.

Systems that establish identity, reputation, and verification will become more important.

---

## 4. Organizations May Change Shape

Companies may evolve from rigid hierarchies into networks of specialists coordinated through AI tools.

Traditional structure:

- departments
- fixed roles
- large employee counts

Emerging structure:

- small teams
- specialized collaborators
- AI-augmented workflows

---

# Skills That Will Matter Most

In an AI-augmented world, valuable skills include:

- judgment
- problem framing
- domain expertise
- taste and quality evaluation
- coordination and leadership

AI makes producing output easier.

Knowing **what should exist** becomes more valuable.

---

# Industries Most Affected

Knowledge work fields will see major impact:

- programming
- research
- writing
- legal analysis
- marketing
- design

Most roles will experience **augmentation first**, not immediate replacement.

---

# Key Insight

Historically, different eras had different constraints.

Industrial era: capital  
Internet era: distribution  
AI era: judgment

AI reduces production costs dramatically, shifting value toward decision making and problem definition.

---

# Personal Observations (Optional to Mention)

AI allows individuals to build tools and systems that previously required teams.

This includes:

- AI-assisted software development
- automated research workflows
- AI-enhanced productivity for solo builders

The barrier to building new systems is falling quickly.
